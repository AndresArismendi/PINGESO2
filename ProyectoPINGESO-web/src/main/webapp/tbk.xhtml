What is T.B.K.?

The approach of similarity of WU-PALMER does not consider that two nodes are in the same hierarchy when they are in a same level of depth in the tree regarding the root node, and in a same vicinity (variation of the concept of vicinity) when they are not in the same hierarchy, and also one is not an ancestor or descendant of the other. With the objective of balancing this consideration, a measure of similarity based on the count of edges, which is a variation of the one presented by WU-PALMER, is proposed, maintaining like this the advantage of the simplicity of it calculation and high performance, by adding a factor that penalizes the similarity between two nodes of the same vicinity. The approach is created to face the problem of semantic identification.
